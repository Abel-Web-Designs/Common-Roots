Meet the Owners

Michaela (mi-k-la): Michaela is a first generation college graduate. She studied logistics for 3 years, and has degree in Human Resource Management. She also is a certified wedding & event coordinator.


About Common Roots

Common Roots is a new small business located in Seymour, Indiana. Common Roots offers a large variety of house plants, Gardening supplies, and plant related books and gifts. Michaela started the business because she believed that she could provide a “new and different” experience for local plant people. The goal is for Common Roots to be your local one stop shop for all plant & gardening needs.

Common Roots would love to hear from you with any comments, questions, or concerns you might have!
